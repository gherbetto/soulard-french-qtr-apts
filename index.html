About

Studio Apartments in the Heart of Soulard.

Discover the freedom and vibrancy of living in a newly renovated studio apartment in the heart of Soulard. Experience the perfect blend of efficiency, economy, and historic charm in a cozy, small-footprint space that feels just right.

Step outside, and you'll find yourself immersed in Soulard's legendary dining, entertainment, and nightlife scene. Duke's—voted St. Louis' favorite bar and top sports bar year after year—is right next door! Iconic spots like McGurk's, Molly's, and live music venues 1860s and Hammerstones are just a short stroll away.

Make Soulard your home and enjoy everything this vibrant neighborhood has to offer!
